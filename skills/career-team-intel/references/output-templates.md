# Output Templates (Evidence-Linked Deliverables)

Keep outputs concise. Prefer 1-page briefs and short messages.

## team_brief.md (one team)

```md
# Team Brief — <Company> / <Team or Group>
Date: <YYYY-MM-DD>
Scope keywords: <...>

## What this team builds (public)
- <1-3 bullets>

## Why now (hiring intent)
- Signal: <strong/medium/weak> — <1 line> (Date, Link)
- Signal: ...

## Priorities & success metrics
- <priority> -> <what “good” looks like> (Link)

## Tech stack & constraints (signals)
- Stack: <...> (Link)
- Constraints: latency/cost/privacy/reliability/... (Link)

## What they likely need in this hire
- <1-5 bullets, each tied to evidence>

## My fit (proof points)
- <proof> -> <metric/result> -> <why it maps> (Link to your work if public)

## Best outreach angle
- <the 1-sentence “why me, why this team” hook>

## Open questions to validate
- <req ID?> <level?> <location?> <interview focus?> ...
```

## hiring_signals.md (timeline)

```md
# Hiring Signals — <Company> / <Team or Group>

| Date | Source | Signal | Strength | Notes | Link |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| YYYY-MM-DD | leader_post | "We're hiring..." | Strong | Ask for req ID | ... |
```

## people_map.md (who to contact)

```md
# People Map — <Company> / <Team or Group>

| Person | Role | Why them | Best channel | What to ask/send | Evidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| <Name> | <EM/TL/HM> | owns hiring | LinkedIn/email | resume + 2 bullets | <links> |
```

## resume_alignment.md (turn intel into bullets)

```md
# Resume Alignment — <Company> / <Team or Group>

| Team priority/pain | Evidence | My proof | Resume bullet (draft) |
|---|---|---|---|
| <latency/cost/etc> | <link> | <metric> | <action + metric + context> |
```

## outreach_messages.md (short, specific, non-creepy)

Leader / hiring manager:
```text
Hi <Name> — I saw your post about hiring for <role/team> (<date>). I’ve worked on <1 matching area> and <2 matching area>, e.g. <metric/result>.
If helpful, I can send a short resume/portfolio; is there a req ID/link you prefer I apply through?
```

IC informational chat:
```text
Hi <Name> — I enjoyed your <talk/blog/repo> on <topic> (<date/link>). I’m exploring roles in <area> and built <proof/metric>.
Would you be open to a 10–15 min chat about what your team values in candidates?
```

## interview_prep.md (from public signals)

```md
# Interview Prep — <Company> / <Team or Group>

## Likely topics (evidence-linked)
- <topic> (Link) -> prepare: <what to say/do>

## My stories (STAR)
- <story title>: Situation / Task / Action / Result (metric)

## Questions to ask them (to validate unknowns)
- “What does success look like in the first 3 months?”
- “Which constraints matter most (latency/cost/privacy/reliability)?”
- “What’s the current bottleneck for the team?”
```
