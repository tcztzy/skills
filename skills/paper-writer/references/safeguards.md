# Safeguards Checklist

Use this checklist before finalizing a manuscript.

## Factuality and evidence
- Every quantitative claim matches experiment logs or summaries.
- No fabricated baselines, datasets, or results.
- Negative or inconclusive results are reported transparently.

## Citation integrity
- Every citation exists and is relevant to the claim.
- No invented references or duplicate entries.
- Related Work includes multiple citations, not just one source.

## Reproducibility signals
- Methods and experimental setup include enough detail to reproduce.
- Hyperparameters and data sources are described or placed in the appendix.

## Ethical and policy checks
- Respect dataset or tool licenses and required acknowledgements.
- Disclose AI assistance if required by venue policy or licensing.
- If the work uses AI-Scientist outputs, include the mandatory AI disclosure required by its license.
- Avoid sharing private or sensitive data.

## Output hygiene
- LaTeX compiles without errors and respects page limits.
- Figures and tables are properly labeled and referenced.
