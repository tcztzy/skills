# Rajpurkar et al. 2018: unanswerable questions and abstention (SQuAD 2.0)

- Title: Know What You Don't Know: Unanswerable Questions for SQuAD
- Authors: Pranav Rajpurkar; Robin Jia; Percy Liang
- Venue/Publisher: Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL 2018, Short Papers)
- Date: 2018-07
- URL: https://aclanthology.org/P18-2124/

## Notes (why this matters for "not answered in the thread")

- Introduces QA evaluation with **unanswerable questions**, where a system should **abstain** rather than guess when the context does not support an answer.
- Frames the task as requiring systems to **identify when no answer is present** in the provided passage (analogous to "thread-grounded" multiple-choice items with an explicit "not answered" option).
