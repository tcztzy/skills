# Strategy Memo

## Core Claim
- One sentence describing what the paper actually claims.

## Impact Thesis
- One sentence answering "so what?" in the strongest truthful form.

## Why Now
- Why this matters now instead of later.

## Why Us
- Comparative advantage, unique setup, or reason this team can do it well.

## Top Risks
- The main technical, novelty, timing, or framing risks.

## Required Evidence
- The minimum experiments, analyses, baselines, or arguments needed next.

## Story Fixes
- Title, introduction, figure, or conclusion fixes needed to make the story land.

## Decision
- Exactly one of: `GO`, `KILL`, `PIVOT`, `SCOPE-DOWN`.
- One sentence on the immediate next action.
