# Oesterle et al., 2017 — COD (Cas9 Online Designer)

Source: Scientific Reports (2017). "Efficient engineering of chromosomal ribosome binding site libraries in mismatch repair proficient *Escherichia coli*." https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-017-12395-3

Used in this skill to support that **COD (Cas9 Online Designer, `cas9.wicp.net`)** has been used for gRNA design and off-target checking.

Relevant excerpt (Methods):

> "The gRNAs for the CRISPR Cas9 system were designed and checked for off-target effects with the web tool COD (Cas9 Online Designer, cas9.wicp.net; ...)."
