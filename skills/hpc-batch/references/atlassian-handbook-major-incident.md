# Atlassian Incident Management Handbook

- Title: The incident management handbook (major incident response)
- URL: https://www.atlassian.com/incident-management/handbook
- Accessed: 2026-02-20

## Relevant points

- Atlassian describes major incidents as customer-facing service disruptions requiring urgent response.
- The handbook notes incidents are often detected through customer reports.

## How this supports the skill

If multiple independent users report the same stuck-processing symptom in the same window, that is consistent with a shared service-side incident signal rather than a single-user input mistake.
