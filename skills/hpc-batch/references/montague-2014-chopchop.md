# Montague et al., 2014 — CHOPCHOP

Source: Nucleic Acids Research (2014). "CHOPCHOP: a CRISPR/Cas9 and TALEN web tool for genome editing." https://doi.org/10.1093/nar/gku410

Used in this skill to support that **CHOPCHOP (`chopchop.rc.fas.harvard.edu`)** is an online tool that predicts off-target binding.

Relevant excerpt (Abstract):

> "We have developed an online tool, CHOPCHOP (https://chopchop.rc.fas.harvard.edu), ... and rigorously predicts off-target binding of single-guide RNAs (sgRNAs) and TALENs."
